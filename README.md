1. Understand and define the problem
   -  **The Bottleneck**:The legacy CRM system suffered from poor industry classification, leaving approximately 50% of the 72,000+ records unidentifiable,duplicated or misclassified.
   -  **Objectives:**
      -  Restructure Unstructured Data: Utilize online tool "Voyant" do Part-of-Speech (POS) tagging and keyword extraction to recover "lost" industry information from company names.
      -  Market Benchmarking: Compare internal customer density with official Government Open Data (DGBAS) to calculate the company's "Market Capture Rate."
      -  Sales Guidance: Visualize data through Heat Maps to identify "High Growth Potential" regions and industries.
   
2. The complexity of Story
   - Dirty Data: Handled a dirty data dataset 72,000+ records after cleaning duplicated and leave out unidentifiable only 28,000+ records able to use.
   - Linguistic Processing: Due to the lack of standardized industry codes, I implemented POS Tagging (Part-of-Speech) logic. For example, extracting categorical keywords like "Logistics" from "ABC Transport Co." or "Construction" from "XYZ Civil Engineering."
   - Inustry alignment: Developed a proprietary 5-tier classification framework (Industrial, Agricultural, Commercial, Professional, and Others) and mapped it to government industrial standards.
